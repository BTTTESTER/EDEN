Attribute: Holy

Rarity: ★★★★★

Weapon Type: Sword

Position: Vanguard

Cost: 40

Noein, who was granted "The Power of Eden" by Cecily, transforms into a more powerful form. While she holds the rank of "Ritter," a "Knight" under Cecily, Noein possesses overwhelming combat abilities due to her innate physical and mental strength, which were present even before her transformation. Her swordsmanship, which was already extraordinary, has now been elevated to the realm of godlike skill, beyond human comprehension.

The Sacred Mark of Astaroth has caused all openings in her body, except for her genitals, to become significantly more sensitive than normal. The sensitivity in her mouth, anus, and urethra has increased many times over. She is vulnerable to sensations in these areas, and even when not transformed, she continues to experience intense reactions during activities such as eating or defecating. However, Noein possesses strong willpower and strives to endure, concealing her discomfort and not showing any weakness to those around her.
