Hildegarde, Empress of Galberia, formed a pact with the ancient holy relic "Holy Corpse Lilith" and transformed into her form as an Edens Ritter.

As compensation for the contract, the "time" that had been offered to Lilith was temporarily returned to her, allowing Hildegarde to assume the form of a mature, beautiful woman, her original appearance as dictated by her true age.

The combined nature of Hildegarde and Lilith is reflected in her new form, resulting in a design that emphasizes draconic elements rather than angelic ones.

She possesses the mobility granted by dragon wings, the defense of dragon scales, the power of dragon might, and even wields offensive magic modeled after a dragon's breath, giving her combat prowess that overwhelmingly surpasses that of other Ritters.

On the other hand, the temptation brought about by the Sacred Mark from Lilith is intensely severe, subjecting both her body and mind to constant pleasure, continuously luring her down the path of corruption. However, with her inherent strong will and confidence, Hildegarde manages to preserve her sense of self.

All the dragon-like features that appear when she transforms are Sacred Mark-bearing parts, her wings, horns, and tail, each of which has become erogenous zones more sensitive than human genitalia.
